NextBday: Birthday Reminders

NextBday makes birthdays effortless. Import dates from Contacts in one tap, pick a daily reminder time, and get an optional heads-up 1–7 days before— so you’re always on time with a message or gift.

Why people love it

  • Instant import from Contacts (no spreadsheets)
  • Daily reminder time you control
  • Advance heads-up (0, 1, 3, or 7 days)
  • Clean, friendly UI with large titles
  • Private by design — birthdays stay on your device

Features

  • “Today” and “Next 7 Days” sections
  • All birthdays by month with quick edit
  • Add/edit names & dates in seconds
  • Local notifications (no accounts needed)

Privacy

  • Uses Contacts only to read name + birthday with your permission
  • No data leaves your device
